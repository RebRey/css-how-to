Breakpoint Containers: .container-{breakpoint}, which is width: 100% until the specified breakpoints.

  • container-sm is 100% wide until small breakpoint
  • container-md is 100% wide until medium breakpoint
  • container-lg is 100% wide until large breakpoint
  • container-xl is 100% wide until extra large breakpoint
  • container-xll is 100% wide until extra extra large breakpoint
